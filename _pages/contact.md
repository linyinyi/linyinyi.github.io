## Collaboration

I am open to collaboration across the full arc of my research — GeoAI and multisource remote sensing methods, urban environmental health, climate risk and community resilience, and sustainable urbanization. I am particularly interested in interdisciplinary projects that connect Earth observation with public health, urban planning, or climate adaptation; in joint grant proposals; and in academic and policy partnerships that put high-resolution spatial evidence to practical use. If any of this overlaps with your work, I would be glad to hear from you.
